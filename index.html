Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikası

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Overview

PilotBoard is a vehicle management app that helps you track fuel consumption, maintenance schedules, driving statistics, budget and expenses, and nearby gas stations. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

In short: PilotBoard does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data you enter into PilotBoard — including vehicle information, fuel records, maintenance logs, driving statistics, and budget/expense data — is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage technologies (Core Data and UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Location Data

PilotBoard may request access to your device's location to provide the following feature:

  • Displaying nearby gas stations on the map

Your location data is used only in real-time to show relevant results and is not stored, recorded, or transmitted to any server. Location access is optional and can be revoked at any time through your device's Settings.

Data Collection

PilotBoard does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Financial information (budget entries, expense amounts)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to the developer

Third-Party Services

PilotBoard uses Apple's MapKit framework to display maps and search for gas stations. This functionality is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are integrated into the app.

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with or sold to any third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the PilotBoard app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app permanently removes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

PilotBoard is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Veri Depolama

PilotBoard'a girdiğiniz tüm veriler — araç bilgileri, yakıt kayıtları, bakım geçmişi, sürüş istatistikleri ve bütçe/harcama verileri dahil — Apple'ın cihaz üzeri depolama teknolojileri (Core Data ve UserDefaults) kullanılarak yalnızca cihazınızda saklanır. Bu veriler cihazınızdan hiçbir zaman çıkmaz ve geliştirici ya da üçüncü taraflar tarafından erişilemez.

Konum Verisi

PilotBoard, aşağıdaki özelliği sağlamak için cihazınızın konum bilgisine erişim isteyebilir:

Konum veriniz yalnızca anlık olarak ilgili sonuçları göstermek için kullanılır; kaydedilmez, depolanmaz veya herhangi bir sunucuya iletilmez. Konum erişimi isteğe bağlıdır ve cihazınızın Ayarlar bölümünden istediğiniz zaman iptal edilebilir.

Veri Toplama

PilotBoard aşağıdakilerin hiçbirini toplamaz:
